Who We Are

We are a Bible-believing Christian fellowship in Madison, seeking to glorify Jesus and serve our city.

Our Vision

To be a Christ-centered community where people from India and every nation can encounter Jesus, grow as disciples, and be equipped to serve.

Our Values

  • Christ at the center of everything
  • Word-based teaching and discipleship
  • Prayer, worship, and dependence on the Holy Spirit
  • Hospitality and genuine fellowship
  • Reaching the next generation and families

What We Believe (briefly)

We hold to the historic Christian faith: One God in three Persons, salvation by grace through faith in Christ, the authority of the Bible, and the call to live holy and loving lives.
